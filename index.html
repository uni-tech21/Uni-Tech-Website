About Uni-Tech

Local support with a professional approach.

Uni-Tech provides repairs and IT support for home users and small businesses. We focus on honest advice, careful workmanship, and results that last.

Whether your laptop is running slowly, you need help removing malware, your files need recovering, or you want a clean website built for your business, we keep the process simple. You tell us what’s happening, we handle the technical side, and we keep you updated throughout.
